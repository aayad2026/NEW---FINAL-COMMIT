What did I achieve Academically?

This semester, was very different academically. Have school online is
a whole new experience. It is no where near the same as having a class in
person because it is just so much easier to get distracted at home, and
the information we get usually just goes in one ear, and out the other.
Though this semester, I feel like I was able to academically hold myself accountable.
Without having a teacher there to make sure that I was paying attention,
I would be hard on myself about doing things I enjoyed. I would make sure all
my work is done, then I would do whatever I want. I also made sure that I
paid attention during the lectures and that I went back and studied on my own
because that is probably the most important thing to do with this online schooling
system. I was able to procrastinate much less this year, and I was about to prove to myself
how capable I am of doing things without as much help as I had access to before.
